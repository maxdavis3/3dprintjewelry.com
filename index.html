SERVICES

Jewelry Printing Process

01

Castable Wax Models

SLA-printed castable resin patterns with clean burnout for investment casting. Exact shrinkage compensation built in. Compatible with all standard casting houses — use your existing caster or our in-house casting service.

02

Direct Metal Printing

18K gold and sterling silver parts printed directly via SLM. Skip the casting step for complex hollow and lattice geometries. Surface finish Ra 3–5µm as-printed, polished to mirror finish on request.

03

End-to-End Production

Full-service from CAD design through casting or direct print to setting, stone-setting, polishing, and hallmarking. Single source for complete jewelry production — prototype to production volumes.

PRODUCTION OPTIONS

Jewelry Production Materials

Two routes to a finished jewelry piece — wax-and-cast for maximum material choice, or direct metal print for maximum geometric complexity.

  • ● Castable resin — zero-ash burnout at 750°C
  • ● 18K yellow, white, rose gold via direct SLM
  • ● Sterling silver 925 and fine silver 999
  • ● Platinum 950 for direct print on specification
  • ● Bronze and brass for fashion and costume jewelry
  • ● CAD design service from sketch to production file
